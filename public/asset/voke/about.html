Who We Are

We're here to Create Connections and Build Growth.

At Tisas, we believe every transaction should be more than just an exchange. It should be an opportunity to create VALUE for businesses and JOY for customers. We've built a platform that turns everyday spending into extraordinary rewards, helping businesses grow while celebrating their customers in meaningful ways.

What We Do

Tisas empowers:
  • Businesses: By providing tools to boost sales, reward customers, and stand out in a competitive market.
  • Customers: By turning every purchase into an opportunity for instant cash, and exciting memorable prizes.

Our Mission

To transform how businesses engage with customers by offering a loyalty solution that is simple, rewarding, and impactful.

Our Vision

To be the leading sales and loyalty platform that bridges businesses and customers, fostering lasting connections and shared growth.

Our Aim

We aim to create a thriving ecosystem where merchants increase sales and customers feel
appreciated with cash rewards and unforgettable experiences.

Our Story

Tisas was born out of a desire to bridge the gap between businesses and customers. We envisioned a platform that unifies loyalty programs across industries, making it easier for businesses to grow and for customers to feel valued. Today, Tisas is not just a loyalty platform—it's a movement that celebrates spending and fosters mutual growth.

Join Us On This Journey

Whether you're a business looking to grow or a customer seeking more from your spending, Tisas is here to make it happen. Together, let's create a world where every transaction brings value, joy, and connection.
