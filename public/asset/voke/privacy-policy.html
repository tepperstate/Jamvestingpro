Privacy Policy for Tisas

At Tisas, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you access our platform, including the website, mobile app, and USSD services. By using Tisas, you consent to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

  1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:
1.1 Personal Information:
  • Name, phone number, email address, and other contact details (as occasion demands).
1.2 Transactional Information:
  • Token purchases, redemptions, and gifted transactions.
  • Cash back and prize draw activity.
1.3 Usage Information:
  • Log data, including IP addresses, device type, browser type, and app usage patterns.
  • Location data, if enabled on your device.
1.4 Marketing Preferences:
  • Information about your preferences for receiving promotional materials and offers.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • To Provide Services: Process token purchases, gifting, redemption, and prize distributions.
  • To Improve User Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance platform functionality.
  • To Communicate: Notify users of token statuses, draw results, and promotional offers.
  • For Security: Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other unlawful activities.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
3. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:
  • With Service Providers: Third-party vendors that support platform operations, such as payment processors and communication services.
  • With Merchants: Transactional data to help merchants track tokens gifted to customers.
  • As Required by Law: To comply with legal requests or enforce our Terms and Conditions.
  • With Consent: If you authorize us to share your information for specific purposes.
4. Data Security
We take data protection seriously and use the following measures to secure your information:
  • Encryption: Data transmitted through our platform is encrypted using relevant and modern protocols.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access your personal information.
  • Monitoring: Regular audits to detect and prevent security breaches.

However, no data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You acknowledge that you use our platform at your own risk.
  1. Your Rights
You have the right to:
  • Update Your Information: Correct inaccuracies in your personal details.
  • Delete Your Information: Request the deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Manage your preferences to decline promotional communications.

6. Data Retention
  • We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil legal obligations. When no longer required, your data will be securely deleted.
7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Our platform uses cookies to:
  • Enhance user experience by remembering preferences.
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns to improve services.

You can manage cookie settings in your browser or device settings.
